Movies Worth Watching

Based on Timi's recommendation

In Time

A sci-fi, action movie from 2011.

It is a social criticism. After watching this film, you will have some topics to think on. On the other hand if you just want to watch something to relax it is a good action movie too.

Life is beautiful

It is an Italian romantic drama from 1997.

What I really like in this film, is how it processes the Holocaust. I think it really has a good humor, however the topic is serious. The title shows this too: a film about the Holocaust, and it says "Life is Beautiful". It's not a bad joke, it is deep enough with the correct humor.

War Room

Inspiring drama from 2015

In this film everything changes after a woman starts to fight her war in praying. I recommend this film because I really agree with what the film is about. As it is in the cover: "Prayer is a powerful weapon".
